About Aeong Crunch

What's a better combination than coffee with cats?

Get ready for a heartwarming surprise as adorable cats welcome you into their home at the Aeong Crunch cafe! This place can be the perfect haven for you, along with some furry friends of different breeds such as Persians, Ragdolls, Shorthairs, and many more! Did you know? Half of our income goes to charity for all cats in different shelters! Come and enjoy yourself a delicious cup of coffee and a scrumptious piece of croissant as our sweet and precious cats cuddle with you!
